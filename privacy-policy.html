Privacy Policy for Yum Yum Tower

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Yum Yum Tower ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Yum Yum Tower does not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from users.

Advertising

Yum Yum Tower uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Leaderboard Scores

If you choose to submit a score to the leaderboard, the name you enter and your score are stored on our servers. We do not associate this with any account or personal identity. You are not required to submit a score.

Local Storage

The app saves game progress and settings locally on your device (high scores, unlocked items, preferences). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Yum Yum Tower is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – advertising platform. AdMob Privacy Policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
