Request Data Deletion – Yum Yum Tower

You can request deletion of any data associated with your use of Yum Yum Tower by contacting us directly via email.

What data we store

The only data stored on our servers is leaderboard entries — the name you chose and your score. We do not store any personal information, account details, or device identifiers on our servers.

All other game data (progress, settings, unlocks) is stored locally on your device only and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

How to request deletion

1 Send an email to framrunner25@gmail.com
2 Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request – Yum Yum Tower
3 Include the leaderboard name you used so we can locate your entry
4 We will delete your data within 7 days and confirm by reply

For questions about our privacy practices, see our Privacy Policy.
